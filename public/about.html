About

A statewide engine for AI innovation.

The NJ AI Hub is a partnership between Princeton University, the New Jersey Economic Development Authority, Microsoft, and CoreWeave — and is anchored on Alexander Road in West Windsor in space provided by Princeton University.

Founded in 2025 with a $72M+ commitment from its founding partners, the Hub brings together AI researchers, industry leaders, startup companies, and other collaborators to advance AI research and development, ethical use of AI for positive social impact, and applied-AI workforce training — in collaboration with New Jersey universities, community colleges, and vocational schools.

Programs at the Hub include a regional AI Accelerator (operated by Plug and Play), Microsoft’s Discovery AI research platform, Microsoft TechSpark workforce training, and a $20M AI Hub Fund from NJEDA and CoreWeave to support innovative startups.
