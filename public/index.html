NJAIHUB

Where New Jersey builds the future of AI.

The NJ AI Hub unites AI researchers, industry leaders, startups, and collaborators to grow the AI ecosystem in New Jersey — enabling world-class research and development, spurring innovation, and training an AI-boosted workforce in collaboration with New Jersey educational institutions.

Our Mission

A statewide engine for AI research, innovation, and workforce training.

Founded by Princeton University, the New Jersey Economic Development Authority, Microsoft, and CoreWeave, the Hub brings together the people, capital, and infrastructure to advance AI research and development, ethical use of AI for positive social impact, and applied-AI workforce training — in collaboration with universities, community colleges, and vocational schools across the state.
